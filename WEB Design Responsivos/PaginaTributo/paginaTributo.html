Michael Jackson

Jackson: pop king

Michael Joseph Jackson (Gary, August 29, 1958 - Los Angeles, June 25, 2009) was an American singer, songwriter and dancer. Nicknamed "King of Pop", he is considered one of the most important and influential cultural figures of all time and one of the greatest artists in the history of music, in addition to being a great humanitarian. Jackson's contributions to music, dance and fashion, along with the promotion of his personal life, have made him a global figure in popular culture for more than four decades.
